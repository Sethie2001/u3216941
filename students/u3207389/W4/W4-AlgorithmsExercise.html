PSEUDOCODE

Oven = 180°C​

Butter = 125g​

Brown sugar = ¼ cup​

Caster Sugar = ½ cup​

Eggs = 1​

Vanilla = 1tsp​

Flour = 1 ¼ cup​

Salt = pinch​

Bicarb = ½ tsp​

Choc Chips = 100g​

Baking time = 10 mins​

​

START​

TURN ON oven ​

LINE baking tray​

CREAM butter and sugars ​

END WHEN light and fluffy​

MIX IN Eggs and vanilla​

END when combined​

MIX IN Flour, salt and bicarb​

END when combined​

MIX IN Choc chips​

END when combined​

DROP tsp on tray 5cm apart​

BAKE​

END WHEN cooked​

COOL​

END​
